Yelp Dataset Project - Understanding Language Patterns in 5-Star Reviews
The goal of this project was to understand common words that are often used in 5-star restaurant reviews on Yelp. Not only was this a natural language processing question, but a question that's answer could allow restaurants to focus on and improve upon certain aspects of their business. We focused on a relatively small dataset of Texas restaurants, and we used a gradient boosting classifier, a random forest classifier, and a logistic regression to further understand the words that carry the most weight in a 5-star review.
Skills: Python, Pandas, NumPy, NLTK, MatPlotLib, Scikit-learn
